Tên sản phẩm
Unmatched: Marvel - For King & Country (New Arrival)
Mô tả sản phẩm
Unmatched is a highly asymmetrical miniature fighting game for two or four players. Each hero is represented by a unique deck designed to evoke their style and legend. Tactical movement and no-luck combat resolution create a unique play experience that rewards expertise, but just when you've mastered one set, new heroes arrive to provide all new match-ups. Unmatched: Marvel - For King & Country features three characters from the Marvel comics universe: Black Widow, Black Panther, and Winter Soldier. Black Widow From the Moscow Protocol to the Budapest Gambit, Black Widow is mission ready. She'll have you caught in her web before you even know she's there. Sidekick: Maria Hill. Winter Soldier A bionic arm and marksman's eye made the boy named Bucky into a lethal fighter. When he was programmed to kill without remorse, then he became the Winter Soldier. Black Panther With his vibranium suit, the stalking panther absorbs your strikes and unleashes them back with his cat-like reflexes. Sidekick: Shuri.
